Welcome to Little Dove Design

Welcome to Little Dove Design.
Our goal is to make your message as beautiful and unique as you are, whether it is a birth announcement for a cherished child, commemorating an event for the most important person in your life, or a wedding invitation that will become a cherished heirloom. You can choose one of our existing designs, or contact us to discuss your ideas. We offer custom illustrations and are happy to work with you to bring your dreams to life. The first step to your big day is letting guests in on all the excitement there is to come! Your invitations will set the overall tone and feel for your special event. We work closely with you to create works of art that will not only be beautiful, but will serve as a lasting keepsake for all of those beautiful memories in years to come. Little Dove Design offers beautiful designs, that are truly classic and elegant and can be custom tailored to fit your ideas, colors and style. Custom illustrations, letterpress printing, foil stamping and digital printing are also offered to fit within an array of budgets. We pride ourselves on exceptional customer service and a warm, friendly attitude. Little Dove Design looks forward to hearing your ideas and building a stunning stationery suite! One that will WOW your guests and have them be as excited as you are. Welcome to Little Dove Design. Let us create something beautiful, timeless, and memorable — just for you.

“What I love most about what I do, is that I get to share in creating this very special event, whether it is a wedding, birth, baptism, communion, bar/batmitzvah, anniversary or birthday. These are all beautiful and very memorable milestones in each person’s life. It brings me great joy knowing that I can play a small role in these cherished moments.”

— Monika Natius: Founder/Creative Director
