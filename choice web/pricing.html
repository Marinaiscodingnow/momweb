Pricing



Please inquire about pricing. We will be happy to furnish you with a quote as we update our pricing list for the year. Our pricing list has three major parts:
1. Our list of over 50 standard ink colors. If you prefer an ink color not on our stock list, please ask, as we can custom mix inks to meet your needs.
2. Pricing for invitation suites. An invitation suite consists of an invitation, the outer envelope, a reply card, and a reply envelope. This is the most commonly ordered set of wedding stationery, so we’ve bundled and priced it first for your convenience.
3. À la carte pricing. In addition to your invitation suite, you can add many individual items and embellishments. We provide a comprehensive list of the elements and decorative options we offer, and their pricing. We now offer digital printing as well, to fit within even the most discerning budgets. Please inquire.
